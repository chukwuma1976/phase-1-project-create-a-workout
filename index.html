Why workout?

There could be many reasons.

  • Weight Loss: exerise WITH a calorie deficit (eating fewer calories than you burn)
  • Recomposition: you may want to gain muscle and lose fat but maintain your body weight
  • Overall Health: at least 150 minutes per week of moderate intensity cardio can reduce mortality
    • AHA guidelines for physical activity
  • Get Stronger: also at least 30 minutes per week of resistance training can reduce mortality
  • Improved appearance: having a lean muscular body can be more attractive
